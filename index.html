Regroup Gathering 2015

As summer begins we invite you to join us in celebration at Regroup Gathering: Conference and Festival from June 19th to 22nd, 2015! Enjoy three nights and days of local and international performers, artists and speakers. Bring in the Summer Solstice with us.

⇑⇓ More Information ⇓⇑

Only 1 ½ hours from Vancouver, Regroup will take place among the splendor of mother nature with a mix of woodland and field in unceded Stó:lō territory.

Nights will be filled with music, dancing and multimedia arts. We will be featuring local and international DJs, music producers, artists, dancers, live musicians and more.

Days will include a conference with a myriad of subjects, workshops, tech talks, creation zones, live performances, kids’ activities for big and small, and of course dancing.

Summer Solstice will find us joining with members of the Stó:lō Nation in celebration of Solidarity Day also known as National Aboriginal Day and sharing stories, games, music and much more.

We are members of many different communities, mainly veterans of festivals and gatherings that have been special experiences for many of you. We have chosen to join together to bring you this gathering.

Celebrate our coming together in all our beautiful diversity and Recharge yourself with this positive energy to take out into the world and your every day.

Camping is included in all tickets. The site has plenty of fresh drinking water. All participants are asked to bring reusable containers. This event is inclusive, all-ages, family-friendly and includes a Kids’ Zone just for the little ones where they can play, create and have fun.

Crew Mini-FAQ:

When will applications close?
Applications will remain open until at least May 1st, 2014... however, to avoid getting too many hopes up, we may close applications early if we feel we have more applications than we do positions available. So don't wait, apply now.
When will I know if I've been accepted?
Crew assignments will take place on an ongoing basis between April 1st and June 1st. If you get an assignment we will contact you by the email you provide. By June 1st at the latest we will send out an email to notify those who we weren't able to find a spot for.
What are the requirements?
Each crew member will be asked to work a minimum of 12 hours throughout the festival, depending on level of involvement (though we always appreciate the superstars who go above and beyond!) In return you get free entry into the festival (there are no crew fees this year). We ask all crew to try to arrive on Thursday, if possible. Depending on your assignment and indicated availability, you may be asked to arrive or leave earlier or later.

While there are no crew fees, certain cells may require a volunteer deposit, to be refunded upon successful completion of work.
What happens if I buy a ticket and then get accepted?
If you bought a ticket and then get accepted you are welcome to resell the ticket, but unfortunately we can't refund it.
First Aid
we are always looking for First Aid crew, especially those with OFA level 3 or higher! If you have a valid first aid certification don't forget to include that in your application. If accepted, you will be required to provide a copy of your certification to your cell head.
Any other questions?
Please email info@entheosgathering.com. (please do not email to apply, or to inquire about application status - unless it is after May 15th and you haven't heard anything).
